Project 2

Comparison between AMD and Intel

AMD vs Intel

This project provides an in‑depth analysis of the differences between AMD and Intel processors, two of the main leaders in the market. It examines aspects such as gaming performance, productivity tasks, multitasking, power consumption, and price‑to‑performance ratio, offering a clear and updated view of the current technological landscape.

It also reviews the evolution of both brands in recent years, how their architectures have changed, and the impact they have had on both consumer and professional markets. The goal is to help users choose the processor that best fits their real needs, whether for gaming, professional work, or low‑power systems.
